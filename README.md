# PocketMentor — “WTF Do I Do With My Money?”

An AI-powered financial mentor for international students trying to manage money, build savings, and make smarter financial decisions in a new country.

---

## 💡 The Problem

As an international student, I found myself earning money but still not getting ahead.

I was working, paying rent, groceries, tuition — real expenses — but I had no system.  
No clear idea of:
- how much to save  
- what to spend  
- when to invest  
- or what to actually do with my money each week  

Most advice online is generic and doesn’t apply to real situations like visa restrictions, limited work hours, and high living costs.

---

## 🚀 The Solution

PocketMentor is a **weekly financial decision system** — not just a budgeting app.

It helps users:
- understand where their money is going  
- identify spending leaks  
- get a clear weekly plan  
- make better financial decisions  
- build consistent saving and investing habits  

---

## 🔥 Key Features

### 📊 Weekly Focus Card
- One rule to follow  
- Exact amount to save  
- Maximum spending limit  

---

### 📈 Weekly Plan
- Money diagnosis  
- What to do this week  
- What NOT to do  
- Save / spend / invest split  
- One clear action  

---

### 💸 “Can I Afford This?”
- Enter a purchase  
- Get YES / WAIT / NO  
- Based on real financial situation  

---

### 🤖 AI Mentor
- Ask real financial questions  
- Get practical, personalized advice  
- Focused on your current situation  

---

### 📉 Money Leakage Tracker
- Shows where money is being lost  
- Compares expected vs actual savings  
- Highlights problem areas  

---

### 📚 Learn Section
- Beginner-friendly financial concepts  
- Budgeting, saving, ETFs, tax basics  
- Focused on international students in Australia  

---
